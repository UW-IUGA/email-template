What is IUGA?

We are the Informatics Undergraduate Association. We are an RSO that acts as the student government for Informatics students. We communicate between the Informatics student body, faculty, and administration of the University of Washington Information School. We host fun, community building events as well as events for professional development. Join a community of over 2000 Informatics prospectives, students, and graduates on our IUGA Facebook Group!
